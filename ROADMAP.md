## üöÄ Feature Ideas

### High Priority
1. **Strategy Marketplace**: Allow users to share strategies
2. **Paper Trading Mode**: Test strategies without real money
3. **Strategy Optimization**: Automated parameter tuning
4. **Risk Analytics**: Advanced risk metrics and visualization
5. **Backtest Comparison**: Compare multiple strategies easily

### Medium Priority
1. **Web Interface**: Browser-based UI for monitoring
2. **Mobile App**: Monitor trades on the go
3. **Alert System**: Notifications for important events
4. **Data Export**: Export results to various formats
5. **Strategy Templates**: Pre-built strategy templates

### Low Priority

5. **API for Third-party**: Allow external integrations
